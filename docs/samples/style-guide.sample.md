# Style Guide (Sample)

## REQUIRED TERMS
- "witcher" should remain lowercase unless part of a title.
- "Kaer Morhen" must remain unchanged.

## FORBIDDEN TERMS
- Avoid "monster hunter"; use "witcher" instead.
- Avoid "magic user"; use "sorcerer" when applicable.

## TONE
- Keep phrasing concise and in-universe.
- Avoid modern slang.
